Please call 1977 from your VoizFonica SIM to complete the tele-verification process. If you wish to activate only Data services, dial 1800-890-1977 from any number. You can tele-verify your number using one of the following options:

    1. Enter 5 Digit PIN received on your alternate number OR
2. Enter last 4 digits of your aadhaar number OR
    3. Reach our contact center advisor for manual verification
After successful verification, services of your VoizFonica SIM will be activated.
